## About Me

Welcome to my website! My name is Julian and I am a final year student undertaking a four year Computer Science degree at the University of Southampton. 

One of my traits is that I love to learn and gain new knowledge about anything and everything that I find interesting. This hunger for learning was sown from a young age, where I vividly remember reading through an encyclopedia set about space and nature for hours, which was merely the starting point of my particular interest in all things science and technology. I especially enjoy these subjects as there seems to be an endless rabbit hole of interesting fields, ideas, and studies that one could immerse themselves into and learn about for their entire lives.

Among all the fascinating things that I've learnt about, I've managed to develop a particular fondness for computers and software engineering ever since I was introduced to programming while I was in high school. This interest was further developed through my university courses, hackathons and game jams that I've participated in with my friends, as well as more professional ventures, such as my recent internship with Revenue Monster, in which I learnt about DevOps and backend API development using Golang and other technologies.

Other than work and school, I love reading books both fiction and non-fiction, among my favourites are ***1Q84*** by *Haruki Murakami* and ***Flowers for Algernon*** by *Daniel Keyes*. Besides that, I've also recently gotten interested in learning about stocks and investing, and enjoyed these books while learning about the subject &mdash; ***The Psychology of Money*** by *Morgan Housel* and ***The Little Book of Common Sense Investing*** by *John C. Bogle*.

During my free time, I also listen to various types of music as well as play video games with my friends. I'm also the Vice-President of Southampton University Kendo Society, and have participated and attained a podium position within an intra-university competition in 2019.
